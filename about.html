About Magazine

The College of Engineering, Trivandrum was established in 1939 as the first college for technical studies in the state of Travancore. The classes commenced on 3rd July 1939, during the reign of Sri Chithira Thirunal Balarama Varma who played a quintessential role in the establishment of the college.


With a legacy of almost 80 proud years, the college has developed and grown and currently functions with 8 fully- fledged departments, offering 8 undergraduate, 19 postgraduate and doctoral programmes, affiliated by K TU. Moreover, the college is a warehouse of new ideas and promotes projects and inven tions of national and international acclaim.


Since, studies are not the only priority of the students of this college, we hope to prove our interest in non- STEM facets of the world and the socio-political and cultural realms of our society through our College Magazine.
